about

Corona virus(covid-19) is an infectious disease caused by the new virus.For more information about corona virus dial *719#

how it spreads

The disease spreads primarily by getting into contact with the infected person. The disease also spreads by touching the contaminated surfaces and then you touch your mouth, nose or eyes.

prevention

There is no vaccine currently that can be used to prevent covid-19

To help stop corona virus you need to do the following

  • Wash your hands regularly for atleast 20 seconds using soap and water or alcohol based hand sanitizer.
  • Maintain a social distance of atleast 1.5 meters
  • Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cough or sneeze.
  • Stay home and avoid unnecessary movements
  • Self-isolate from other when feeling unwell or experiencing cpovid-19 like sysmptoms
  • Avoid touching your nose, mouth and eyes

cure

Currently there is no medicine or vaccine for covid-19, people may only be taken care of to help them.

Dial 719 for covid-19 suspected cases
